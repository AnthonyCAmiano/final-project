Stop the Spending Spree and Effectively Manage the County's Debt

Westchester County is taking on too much debt. This constant borrowing of funds will have to be paid for by our children and grandchildren down the road. We cannot allow the out-of-control spending by the Board of Legislators to continue. While other County departments had to slash spending by 10-20%, the Board of Legislators' budget actually increased! Common sense tells you that when the revenue stream is not growing, expenses should not be growing either. Just as every family has had to "tighten-the-belt" during these tough times, so too should the Board of Legislators.
