November 10, 2011- Numerous media outlets have declared Michael Smith the unofficial winner of the District 3 race! Hundreds of ballots remain, meaning anything can happen. The Board of Elections plans to declare an official winner over the next few weeks, late next week being the earliest and the end of the month being the latest. Stay tuned for more updates. November 9, 2011- At this point, the District 3 County Legislator race is too close to call. The Westchester County Board of Elections has impounded all voting machines and paper ballots until further notice. At this time, both Michael Smith and John Nonna are tied with 50% of the vote, Nonna pulling slightly ahead with a +60 vote advantage. However, this number does not account for seven (7) remaining districts still not accounted for and for the paper, absentee ballots which total over 200. After all the legislative elections, the Democratic stranglehold on our county government is still in tact, with this seat being the deciding one. Michael Smith hopes he can break that stranglehold with a victory. Stay tuned to this page as well as Michael's Facebook page,www.facebook.com/smithforlegislator, for updates as they come in.
